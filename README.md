### 📊 ADIDAS SALES ANALYSIS DASHBOARD

## 1. Project Overview
The Adidas Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze sales performance across regions, states, retailers, products, and time periods. The dashboard provides actionable insights to support business decision-making and improve sales performance.

## 2. Problem Statement
Adidas operates across multiple regions and retailers, generating large volumes of sales data. Management requires a centralized reporting solution to monitor performance, identify trends, and support strategic planning.

## 3. Objective
Analyze overall sales performance.
Identify top-performing regions and states.
Evaluate retailer performance.
Analyze product category sales.
Monitor monthly sales trends.
Support data-driven business decisions.

## 7. Dashboard Components
Sales Trend Analysis
Displays monthly sales performance and helps identify seasonal patterns and peak sales periods.
Sales by State
Shows sales contribution from different states, helping identify high-performing markets.
Sales by Region
Compares sales across geographic regions to understand regional performance.
Sales by Product
Highlights best-selling product categories and their revenue contribution.
Sales by Retailer
Evaluates retailer performance and identifies key revenue-generating partners.
Interactive Filters
Region Filter
Invoice Date Filter
These filters allow users to analyze data dynamically.

## 8. Key Insights
Regional Performance
West region generated the highest sales contribution.
Midwest region recorded the lowest sales.
Product Performance
Men's Street Footwear emerged as the top-selling product category.
Apparel products also contributed significantly to total revenue.
Retailer Performance
West Gear generated the highest sales.
Foot Locker and Sports Direct were among the top-performing retailers.
Sales Trends
July recorded the highest sales.
March showed comparatively lower sales performance.
Sales recovered strongly toward the end of the year.

## 9. Business Recommendations
Increase inventory for top-performing product categories.
Expand marketing efforts in lower-performing regions.
Strengthen partnerships with high-performing retailers.
Plan promotional campaigns during low-sales periods.

## 10. Conclusion
The Adidas Sales Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance across regions, states, products, and retailers. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor business performance efficiently and make informed strategic decisions.
